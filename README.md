# Dairy-Milk-Diary
An android System to manage the milk collection and link both the milk collector and the farmer. 
The app comprises of a Milk Collector and Milk Farmer platform
The Milk collector can update his or her farmers on how much amount of milk they have delivered on a particular day and their payment records. The Collector has the privelledge of updating his list of farmer at Realtime.
The Milk farmer can view his or her records via the system.
